About

Hi, my name is Chris Steele, and I am an aspiring Data Scientist. I’ve been working as an Applications Engineer for 6 years, but over the last few years, I’ve become more and more attracted to the fields of Data Science and Analytics. It began with me leading a work project relating to Big Data Analysis. While working on this project, I learned from the ground up how to program using Python, connect to SQL databases to query them, and create detailed visualizations that communicate complicated information in a simple way. This project was a huge success and ignited a passion for me in the field of Data Science. Since working on this project, my company has continued to give me Data Analytic related projects, as they knew I have an interest in them, and they were happy with the added value to company. The projects I’ve taken on range from automating standard processes the Applications Engineering group performs, to aggregating warranty data from multiple customers to create a new monthly reporting structure.

I’ve now realized that my passion is in Data Science and Analytics, and I am transitioning my career accordingly. I hope to find a position in the field soon, and I am excited for what the future holds.
